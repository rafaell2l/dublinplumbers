All gas and oil boilers we fit are “A” rated. For honest advice on the best brand for your home, just give Nick a call on 086 237 6237.

Gas boiler servicing

  • Visual check of flue ways and terminal
  • Combustion air requirements check
  • Working pressure check at meter and appliance
  • Gas rate check for the appliance
  • Gas tightness test
  • Standing pressures check
  • Pilot injectors and seals check
  • Cleaning of fan and burner assembly
  • Flue flow test & spillage test

Oil boiler servicing

A full visual inspection checks for oil leaks in tank, pipes and fittings, signs of corrosion in the flue or boiler parts, and any cracks in the firebox or target wall. The draft regulator is checked to move freely.

On the boiler itself: oil shut-off valve operation, flame failure device, draught stabilizer and burner refractory are inspected. Fuel filter and burner nozzle are replaced; spark electrodes, burner diffuser, flame photocell and air tube are cleaned.

A combustion efficiency test is performed, along with a thorough inspection of the whole heating system — thermostats, zone valves and radiators included.
